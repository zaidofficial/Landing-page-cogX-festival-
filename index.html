Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about the CogX Festival

What is the CogX Festival?

The CogX Festival is one of the world’s largest gatherings of leaders, innovators, and researchers in AI and emerging technologies. It brings together experts from business, government, and academia to shape the future of technology.

When and where will the festival take place?

The 2025 edition of the CogX Festival is expected in September at the O2 Arena, London, UK.

Who can attend the festival?

The festival is open to tech professionals, students, researchers, entrepreneurs, investors, and anyone interested in the future of AI, innovation, and technology.

How can I register for the event?

You can register through the official CogX website. Early bird tickets and group discounts are often available, so make sure to book in advance.

Will there be online participation options?

Yes, the CogX Festival usually provides hybrid options, allowing participants to join virtually and access recorded sessions after the event.

What kind of topics will be covered?

Topics include Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, Climate Tech, Future of Work, HealthTech, Startups, Investment, and policy discussions on responsible innovation.
